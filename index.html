The Elegance Flavor History

More than 25+ years of experiance

Elegance Flavor, established in 2009, has proudly written its chapter in fine dining. Founded by visionary chef Sozan Hama Kaka, the restaurant was born out of a deep love for exceptional cuisine and a commitment to delivering unparalleled dining experiences.


From its modest beginnings, Elegance Flavor has blossomed into a revered culinary destination. With a dedication to sourcing the finest ingredients, innovative menu creations, and a focus on impeccable service, we've earned the admiration of food enthusiasts. Today, Elegance Flavor stands as a testament to the enduring pursuit of culinary excellence, inviting you to join us on a journey through flavors that define sophistication.
